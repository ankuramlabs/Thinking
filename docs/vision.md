# Thinking Vision

Thinking is intended to become a structured knowledge operating system for ideas, research, engineering, architecture, product strategy, and decision making.

## Goals

- Capture ideas from first principles.
- Build reusable frameworks.
- Document architectural decisions.
- Enable AI-assisted knowledge management.
- Evolve into a searchable knowledge graph.

## Long-term Direction

Thinking should serve as the foundation for an AI-native knowledge platform that combines notes, research, documentation, and execution into one coherent system.
